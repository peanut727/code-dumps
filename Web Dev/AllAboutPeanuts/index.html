Hazelnuts

Hazelnuts, also known as filberts, are edible nuts that come from the hazel tree. They have a hard outer shell and a creamy, slightly sweet kernel inside. Hazelnuts are widely used in both sweet and savory dishes, including confectionery, baking, and cooking. They are found in chocolates, spreads like Nutella, cakes, cookies, and pastries. Hazelnuts are not only delicious but also nutritious, being a good source of healthy fats, vitamins (such as vitamin E and B6), minerals (like magnesium and potassium), and dietary fiber.

Pistachio

Pistachio nuts are prized for their unique flavor, vibrant green color, and satisfying crunch. These small, tear-shaped nuts are versatile and can be enjoyed as a snack or used as an ingredient in a variety of dishes. With a slightly sweet and rich taste, pistachios add a delightful texture and distinct flavor to recipes. They are also packed with protein, healthy fats, fiber, and essential nutrients, making them a nutritious choice for a balanced diet. Whether eaten on their own or incorporated into culinary creations, pistachio nuts are a beloved and flavorful treat.
